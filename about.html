Who We Are

Redefining Agriculture & Food Systems

SABRI (Sustainable Agriculture Bioscience Research & Innovation Pvt. Ltd.) is a visionary initiative aimed at redefining agriculture and food systems in India. Built on a strong foundation of bioscience, agriculture, and food technology, the organization focuses on solving critical challenges such as low farm profitability, post-harvest losses, limited processing infrastructure, and weak market linkages.

We actively promote high-value horticulture crops, hydroponics, and modern farming techniques that increase productivity and farmer income while ensuring sustainability. At the same time, SABRI supports entrepreneurs in establishing food processing units that convert raw agricultural produce into value-added products.

Bihar is an agriculture-based state. From the industry point of view, the most potential in Bihar is of agriculture-based industry. So food processing industry has the highest potential in Bihar. Our primary objective is to deliver organic products — produced by farmers through Natural farming methods — to markets both within the country and abroad.

The company also works as a facilitator under government schemes, providing support in project planning, DPR preparation, licensing, training, branding, and marketing. Our approach is based on the concept of “Bhoomi (Soil) to Bhojan (Plate)”, ensuring a seamless integration of production, processing, and consumption.

The Problem

Challenges We Address

Indian agriculture faces critical hurdles impacting both farmers and the food system.

Market Inaccessibility

Farmers struggle with direct access to markets, leading to lower profits and dependency on middlemen.

Post-Harvest Losses

Significant produce is wasted due to poor logistics and lack of processing infrastructure.

Supply Chain Inefficiencies

Weak linkages between farm, processing, and market result in economic losses across the chain.
