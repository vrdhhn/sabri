Founder & CEO

Founder & CEO Profile

Rakesh Priyadarshi is a Biotechnology professional with over 10 years of multidisciplinary experience across agriculture, food processing, and public health nutrition. He holds a B.Tech in Biotechnology from Dr. Rajendra Agricultural University, along with an M.Sc. in Botany and a B.Ed. in Biological Sciences, complemented by advanced qualifications in Diet & Therapeutic Nutrition and Public Health Program Management.

He has worked with leading institutions including the Centre of Advanced Climate Change (Dr. RPCAU, Pusa), Department of Horticulture and Department of Industries, Government of Bihar, and has contributed to projects under the Ministry of Food Processing Industries, Government of India. His professional journey includes hands-on experience as a Plant Tissue Culture Lab Incharge in the sugar industry, along with involvement in innovative projects such as in-silico drug design and the “Green Link Alliance – Bhoomi to Bhojan” initiative.

With participation in over 50 trainings, workshops, and national-level programs like World Food India, he brings strong expertise in entrepreneurship development, agri-innovation, and food value chain advancement.
