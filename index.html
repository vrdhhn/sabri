About SABRI

Sustainable Agriculture Bioscience Research and Innovation (SABRI) Private Limited is a pioneering company committed to transforming the agricultural ecosystem through innovation, sustainability, and technology-driven solutions. Recognized under Startup India (DPIIT), SABRI is leading a transformative initiative, “Green Link Alliance – Bhoomi to Bhojan,” aimed at creating a seamless integration between farmers, markets, and consumers.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between farm-level practices and advanced bioscience technologies, ensuring better productivity, improved farmer income, and healthier food systems.
