About SABRI

SABRI (Sustainable Agriculture Bioscience Research & Innovation Pvt. Ltd.) is a visionary initiative aimed at redefining agriculture and food systems in India. Built on a strong foundation of bioscience, agriculture, and food technology, we focus on solving critical challenges through sustainable farming and food processing.
