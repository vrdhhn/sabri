Core Approach

Innovation & Work Model

How SABRI drives sustainable change through research, capacity building, enterprise support, and market linkage.

Pillar 1

Research & Innovation

Development of bio-based and sustainable agricultural technologies for a productive future.

Hydroponics & Soil-less Farming

Sustainable, water-efficient cultivation for high-value exotic crops. Reduces resource usage while delivering premium produce with consistent quality.

Probiotic & Functional Food Research

Research and development focused on health-forward food products — including probiotic foods, millets, and nutritional value-added items.

High-Value Crop Selection

Expert guidance on exotic, medicinal, and high-value crops — including Moringa, Mushrooms, Makhana, and specialty vegetables for maximum farm income.

Health Counselor — Diet & Nutrition

Bridging agriculture and human health by promoting nutritious food systems aligned with sustainable production of health-enhancing crops and products.

Pillar 2

Capacity Building & Training

Empowering farmers, entrepreneurs, and FPOs with the skills and knowledge to succeed.

Farmer Skill Development

Comprehensive skill development programs for farmers, entrepreneurs, and Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs).

Food Processing Training

Hands-on training in food processing and value addition — turning raw produce into marketable, higher-income products.

Technical Workshops

Practical workshops on modern agriculture techniques, food safety, hygiene, and entrepreneurship development.

Pillar 3

Enterprise Development Support

End-to-end guidance for setting up and scaling food processing and agri-enterprises under government schemes.

Food Processing Unit Setup

Assistance in setting up small and medium-scale food processing units — from plant layout to machinery selection and operations guidance.

DPR Preparation

Professional Detailed Project Report preparation for subsidy applications, bank loans, and government scheme facilitation.

Licensing & Compliance

Support in obtaining necessary licenses and maintaining regulatory compliance for food businesses and processing units.

Government Scheme Facilitation

Expert consultancy for accessing government subsidies, schemes, and startup support programs for agri-entrepreneurs.

Pillar 4

Market Linkage & Branding

Connecting farm produce to markets through strategic branding, packaging, and distribution channels.

Product Development & Packaging

Developing market-ready products with professional packaging solutions that enhance shelf appeal and consumer trust.

Branding & Marketing

Building strong brand identities for agricultural products and processed foods through strategic marketing support.

B2B, B2C & Export

Connecting producers to retail and institutional buyers through B2B and B2C channels, with future export opportunities.

Pillar 5

Processed Foods with FPO Partnerships

Scaling impact through value-added processing and strategic community partnerships.

FPO Strategic Alliances

Leveraging Farmer Producer Organization (FPO) partnerships to create a robust supply chain that ensures higher farmer profitability and consistent raw material quality.

Value-Added Processing

Focusing on converting raw agricultural produce into premium processed foods, enhancing shelf life and creating high-value market opportunities.

Impact Focus

Greater Farmer Profitability & Rural Empowerment
